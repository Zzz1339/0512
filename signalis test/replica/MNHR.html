Mynah Unit

MNHR (Minenarbeit-, Nukleartechnik-, Hochsicherheits-Replika), (Mining, Nuclear Tech, High-Security Replika 'Mynah') or Mynah units are Generation 3 Industrial Specialist Replikas. These units are deployed in hazardous work environments across the Eusan Nation, including in the Sierpinski mining operation.
